Description Information

Interchange Locations
TO CSXT: Tilford Yard - Atlanta, GA TO NS: Inman Yard - Atlanta, GA
Actual Interchange Reporting
Interchange reporting will be completed within four (4) hours of the event.
Revision of ISA
Each party to this ISA will notify the other at least fourteen (14) days in advance of the need to revise the ISA. The reason(s) for the revision and the plan for the revision will be presented to the partner in writing. Each partner must agree in writing before the ISA will be considered revised and the revision date added to the ISA. The Service Design/Planning groups on each railway will coordinate and execute the revisions to the ISA as necessary. Should the parties be unable to agree on mutually satisfactory revisions within thirty (30) days of the date of the notice of demand for revision, either party may terminate this ISA on an additional (30) days' notice to the other party hereto.
Performance Evaluation
Service Design/Planning groups within each railroad will measure the performance of this ISA and jointly review the results quarterly or semi-annually.
Advance Consist Requirements
Roads to forward advance notification and consists four (4) hours prior to interchange. AEI readers will be used to augment this consist information.
Temporary and Emergency Plans
CSXT Operations Center and NS Network Operations Center will coordinate emergency plan overrides. Service Design/Network Planning groups on both railways will jointly define and measure all time period exemptions from the plan. Either party may revise the schedule, upon 24 hours notice, when temporary or emergency conditions exist. The parties agree to adjust the ISA if emergent conditions continue to exist. The parties also agree to exchange Holiday Operating Plans and engineering curfew plans with as much advance notice as practical. CSX D-OC (904)359-7550.
Agreement Conflicts
In the event any provision(s) included in this Interline Service Agreement conflicts with provision(s) contained in other legally executed agreements, the provision(s) contained in such agreements will govern.
Mechanical Inspection Requirements
AAR Interchange Rules, and Provisions of the governing Interchange Agreement apply. Receiving carrier performs standard interchange inspection.
Interchange Event
NS to CSXT: Upon arrival at CSXT Tilford yard on designated interchange tracks, engines cut away and 418 and proper documentations is received CSXT to NS: Upon arrival at NS Inman Yd on designated interchange tracks, engines cut away and 418 and proper documentations is received NOTE: Local operating managers have agreed that crews of the offering carrier must have (3) three hours of service remaining under hours of service rules when arriving at I/C juntion. Further the receiving carrier will not be penalized for refussing to accept the interchange consist.
Locomotive Requirements
Locomotive requirements will be covered in the Master Locomotive Agreement
