To assist you with completing an application, you will need the required information readily available. See the Recipient Qualifications and Requirements Checklist  (109kb).

Once you have completed minimal required information, you will be given a Reference ID #. Use the Reference ID # to complete an in-progress application, or once submitted, check the status of your application.

Upon completion of the Online Enrollment Application, signature pages will need to be mailed to finalize the enrollment process. You will be given instructions for mailing in the necessary documents at the end of the Online Enrollment Application.

When you are completing an Individual or Organization Recipient Enrollment application, you will be given the option to also enroll as a Primary Care Recipient (PCP) in the Community Care of North Carolina/Carolina ACCESS (CCNC/CA) program if your Recipient type qualifies you to participate. See CCNC/CA Eligible Recipient Types for more details.

Purpose: To collect information required for the determination of ADAP/HIV Medications Program eligibility. An interviewer completes this form when a service authorization is requested unless a current form is already on file. Once determined, eligibility generally extends for 15 months. A new form is required when changes in countable family members and/or income occur.

Preparation: Consult Purchase of Medical Care Services manual for information on residency requirements, income calculation and expense documentation. Income may be entered in the column labeled "Gross Income" or the one labeled "Income After Taxes". The same income should not be entered in both columns. Both Net and Gross Income need to be completed for ADAP.
